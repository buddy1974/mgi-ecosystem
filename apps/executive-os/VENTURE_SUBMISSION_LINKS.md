## Telegram Commands (power users)

Format: `/report [venture] [urgency] [title] | [details]`

Urgency options: `normal` · `urgent` · `critical`

Type prefixes: `/report` · `/update` · `/incident` · `/approval`

**Venture slugs:** `rogers` · `horeb` · `nmi` · `lq` · `dros` · `abba` · `mgi`

**Examples:**
```
/report horeb urgent New Client Contract | We have a 5M XAF contract pending CEO signature.
/incident dros critical Site Accident | Worker injured at Bonanjo site. Ambulance called.
/approval lq normal New Supplier | Need CEO approval for 2M XAF fabric supplier deal.
/report nmi normal Weekly Summary | Enrollment up 12% this week. 3 new students onboarded.
```
